About Me
Hi, I’m Rutvik Patel — a dedicated and curious IT professional with a passion for building and testing software that works flawlessly. I’m currently completing two post-graduate diplomas at Fanshawe College: Software and Information Systems Testing and Technical Systems Analysis. My learning journey is backed by hands-on experience in test automation, web development, and Agile project collaboration.

I’ve worked on a variety of projects — from automating tests for full-stack applications using Selenium and TestNG, to developing secure booking and e-commerce platforms using PHP, MySQL, and JavaScript. I also enjoy working with tools like Jira, Postman, JMeter, and Git, and I have a growing interest in AWS and DevOps fundamentals.

My strengths lie in problem-solving, attention to detail, and a drive to constantly improve. Whether it’s writing test scripts, designing UI/UX in Figma, or managing test cycles in an Agile setup, I’m always eager to take on new challenges and learn from every opportunity.
